⚡ Recent Trend

Forest loss has shown a significant upward trend in the RFO buffer area, reaching a record high of 45,843 hectares in 2025. This acceleration in deforestation highlights the urgent need for enhanced monitoring and conservation interventions in the expanded buffer zones.

🌳 About OWR

The Okapi Wildlife Reserve protects over 13,700 km² of pristine rainforest in northeastern DRC, home to the endangered okapi and countless other species.

📊 Data Source

Global Forest Watch (GFW) provides satellite-based forest monitoring data. Analysis covers 2001-2025 with annual updates.
